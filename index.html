I am Superman

Introduction

Superman, also known as Clark Kent or Kal-El, is a superhero from the planet Krypton. He was sent to Earth as a baby and raised by human parents. With powers like super strength, flight, and heat vision, he fights for truth, justice, and the American way. Other Superhero

Iron Man, also known as Tony Stark, is a genius inventor who wears a suit of powered armor. This armor grants him superhuman strength, flight, and a variety of advanced weaponry. He also has a brilliant mind for technology and strategy.
Spider-Man, also known as Peter Parker, has superhuman strength, agility, and the ability to cling to walls. He also has a "spider-sense" that alerts him to danger and can shoot webs from his wrists
: Wonder Woman, or Diana Prince, possesses superhuman strength, speed, and agility. She can fly and has exceptional combat skills.Her weapons include the Lasso of Truth, indestructible bracelets, and a magical sword.
